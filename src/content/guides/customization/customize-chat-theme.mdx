## Introduction

Customize the look and feel of your chat interface by selecting or creating a unique theme.  
This guide explains how to choose from preset themes or create a custom theme with your preferred colors and transparency.


## How to Customize Your Chat Theme

### **Access the Theme Settings**  
   From the sidebar, click on your user profile area to open the dropdown menu. Select _Theme_ to open the theme selection modal.

     src="/guides/select-theme.png"

### **Choose or Customize a Theme**  
   In the theme selection modal, choose from six preset themes: _Moonlight Shadow_, _Ocean Blue_, _Sunset Orange_, _Mint Green_, _Cotton Pink_, or _Lavender Haze_.  
   Alternatively, select custom colors and adjust transparency to create a personalized theme.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many preset themes are available?**  
A: There are six preset themes: Moonlight Shadow, Ocean Blue, Sunset Orange, Mint Green, Cotton Pink, and Lavender Haze.

**Q: Can I create a custom theme?**  
A: Yes, you can select custom colors and adjust transparency in the theme selection modal to create a unique theme.
