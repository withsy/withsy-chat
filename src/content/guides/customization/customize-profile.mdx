## Introduction

Personalize your AI model by setting a custom name and profile image.  
This guide walks you through the steps to make your AI interactions more unique and engaging.


## How to Customize Your AI Model Profile

### **Go to the Models Section**  
   Log in and navigate to the *Models* section from the sidebar.

   <img
     src="/guides/models-section.png"
     alt="Models Section Screenshot"
     width="600"
     height="400"
     className="my-6 rounded-lg shadow-sm w-full max-w-md mx-auto"
   />

### **Edit Profile**  
   Click the profile image area to upload and crop a new image; the change will apply immediately without needing to click *Save*.  
   To change the name, use the input field and click *Save* to apply the update.  
   You can also click *Remove Custom Image* to revert to the default image.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What image formats are supported?**  
A: You can upload PNG or JPEG images, with a size limit of 1MB.

**Q: How long can the model name be?**  
A: Model names can be up to 20 characters long.

**Q: Can I revert to the default profile?**  
A: Yes, click *Remove Custom Image* in the edit profile section to restore the default image.
